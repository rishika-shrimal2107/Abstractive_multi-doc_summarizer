Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who are we and what do we do?

    We are a team of open source contributors who have created this website "SmartSnip" with the aim to provide a one step solution to lengthy readings and manually writing summaries of long text/documents We hope that everyone can quickly grasp the main points without reading every word with our SmartSnip.

  • How do I use SmartSnip?

    To use SmartSnip, follow these steps:
    1. Enter your text by typing or pasting it into the provided area.
    2. Adjust the size of your summary as needed.
    3. Click on the "Summarize" button.
    4. Once the summary is generated, you can copy it to the clipboard and use it anywhere.
